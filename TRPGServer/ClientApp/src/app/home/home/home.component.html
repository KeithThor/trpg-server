About the Game

This is a Massively-Multiplayer Online Tactical Role-Playing Game. Think old school Final Fantasy but multiplayer and with a strategic twist. You may create as many characters as you want, however, you may only battle using up to 9 characters at a time.

Battles are fought in Formations which are 3 x 3 grids. Positioning is key to victory in battle. Place your beefier and more heavily armored characters on the right-most column to protect your squishier characters. If your front-line is defeated, the enemy can start attacking your mid-line and back-line characters. Ranged and magical attacks can attack any position in your Formation, however.

Pay attention to the details about your abilities. Some abilities cannot target pass any enemies, while some let you ignore the positioning of the enemy's Formation completely. Some abilities cannot move their target position in a Formation, but will allow you to target other Formations. Finally, some abilities use your own character as their target position, these are called point blank abilities.

You can move around the World Map by right-clicking on a tile. If you right-click a tile with a character on it, the context menu will appear and give you choices on what you want to do. You can move to other maps by moving on top of a door tile.

Battles are turn-based, which means all players and ai on one team all take their turns at the same time. Then their enemys all take their turns afterwards. The players or ai who started the battle get to take their turns first. At the start of the turn, your 5 able-bodied characters with the highest Action Points are able to act. Battles go on until one side has no living characters left.
