Attacks are a mostly physical means of dealing damage to your enemies. They cost no resources to perform and usually cost less action points to perform than other actions. Different attacks are available for different weapons.
Defending allows one character to pass its turn over to other characters who have more than 0 action points. It also significantly reduces the damage the defending character will take when hit. A defending character cannot receive a free turn from another character who defends.
Spells are magical means of dealing damage and applying status effects. They usually cost mana to cast and are usually higher in action point cost than other actions. A character will not be able to cast spells if silenced.
Skills are special physical actions your character can perform to deal damage or apply status effects. They don't cost any mana to perform, although they cost more action points to use than Attacks. Unlike Spells, a character can still use skills if silenced.
A character can use consumable items and any equipment it has equipped that can be activated here.
You can use up this character's turn to attempt to flee combat after your enemy's turn. Every character set to flee will increase your chances of fleeing combat by 20%. If a fleeing character is stunned, it will reduce your chances of fleeing by 20%.
