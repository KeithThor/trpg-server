Growth Points

This section allows you to allocate points into stats of your choosing. These are not representative of your character's actual stats, instead these represent the chance your character has of gaining that stat point whenever it levels up.

Green crystals represent your allocated points.

Cyan crystals represent bonus points given by your race and sex.

Red crystals represent point reductions given by your race and sex.

Gray crystals represent the maximum amount of points you can invest in a stat given your race and sex.

Edit Character

You can choose any of your previously created characters and edit their name, hair, and growth points freely. Their race and sex are locked however.

Strength

Strength is a measure of a character's physical prowess. The stat affects the amount of damage dealt with melee weapons and physical skills.

Dexterity

Dexterity is a measure of a character's physical prowess. The stat affects the amount of damage dealt with certain melee and ranged weapons and the damage of a physical critical strike. Also increases the effectiveness of skills that use dexterity as a base.

Agility

Agility is a measure of a character's ability to move about quickly. The stat affects the likelihood of landing a critical hit. Agility is also used to determine which character acts first in a round, with the highest agility character acting first. A character with very high agility may be allowed to act multiple times in a round.

Intelligence

Intelligence is a measure of a character's mental acruity. The stat affects the amount of damage dealt with magical attacks with staves, wands, and spells. It also increases the amount of mana a character has, allowing it to cast more spells.

Constitution

Constitution is a measure of a character's physical endurance. The stat affects the amount of hit points a character has, allowing it to take more damage before fainting. It also increases the effectiveness of skills and spells that use constitution as a base.
